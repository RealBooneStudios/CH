                    <p class="text-gray-700 mb-4">Primarily **Males 26-65** who value American-made products and lead a lifestyle associated with work wear, blue-collar professions, and outdoor activities ("people willing to get dirty").</p>

                    <p class="text-gray-700">Their values include quality, durability, tradition, comfort, practicality, and supporting local/American businesses. The socks solve problems like uncomfortable shoes, cold feet, lack of style options, and the need for durable work socks.</p>
